at various scales from the city to individuals objects since it localises information spatially and links it to a given morphological being appears as one of the possible answers It seems besides well fitted to meet the needs of a discipline the architectural heritage where such questions as data uncertainty in reconstructions or re use of objects are present However 3D representation today seems
